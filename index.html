Mensa-UPB

A simple application for quering the meals for the mensa (and other facilities) at University Paderborn.

View project onGitHub

Welcome to Mensa UPB.

Mensa UPB is a simple GPL licensed Android application to display the meals of the mensa at the University of Paderborn on a weekly base.

Roadmap

Following features are planned for the future:

  • Display opening time
  • Display static meals (e.g. for One-Way-Snack)
  • Provide better response when reloading data
  • Play Store Publication
